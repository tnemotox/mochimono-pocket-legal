App Review Notes

1. Overview
Mochimono Pocket is a checklist app designed for parents in Japan to manage their children's belongings for various occasions (school trips, pool days, daily commute, hospital visits, park outings, overnight trips, and sports days).

2. Offline-Only App
This app operates entirely offline. It does not connect to the internet, does not make any network requests, and does not require any backend server. All data is stored locally on the device.

3. No Authentication Required
There is no login, sign-up, or authentication of any kind. Users can start using the app immediately after download. No demo account is needed for review.

4. No In-App Purchases or Advertisements
Version 1.0.0 does not include any in-app purchases, subscriptions, or advertisements. All features are completely free.

5. Testing Instructions
- Launch the app
- On first launch, you will see an onboarding screen prompting family registration
- Register a family member (name and birthdate) or skip this step
- From the home screen, tap "New Checklist" to create a checklist
- Select a scene (e.g., "School Trip")
- Select participating family members
- Set conditions (weather, temperature, transportation, duration)
- A checklist will be automatically generated with appropriate items
- Items are organized into "Night Before" and "Morning Of" tabs
- Check off items as you pack them
- You can add or remove items from the generated list
- View the "Diff" screen to see changes from the template
- Save customizations as "Family Rules" for future use
- Access past checklists from the History tab

6. Privacy
The app collects no personal data. Family information (children's names and birthdates) is stored only on the device and never transmitted externally. No analytics or tracking SDKs are included in v1.0.0.

7. Target Audience
Parents and caregivers of infants, preschoolers, and elementary school children in Japan.

8. Language
The app UI is in Japanese only (v1.0.0).
